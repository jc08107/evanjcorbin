Asher & the Prince: The Apex Blade

YA · Fantasy · Sci-Fi · LGBTQ

In a world where ancient magic and lost history collide, Asher Snow, a commoner with a sharp mind and a heavy heart, finds himself on a path he never intended to tread. After losing his last coin in a tavern game, Asher's luck seems to change when he saves a witch's life and is granted a single wish. Driven by a desperate desire to protect his kingdom, Asher wishes for Prince Hunter Bracken to fall in love with him, hoping it will avert an impending war.

But not everything is as it seems. The witch manipulates both Asher and the prince into seeking a legendary sword that could end all wars. As they embark on a dangerous quest across a land fraught with enchantments and dark secrets, Asher and the prince discover a connection neither expected—one that could change the fate of their world.
